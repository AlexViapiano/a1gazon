Painting

At A Plus, we provide complete interior residential and commercial painting all across the Montreal area. Whether you have a small apartment, a large home, or an office, the A Plus team strives for excellence and will ensure your property looks its finest.

Varnishing

One of our main specialties is staining and varnishing. We do it all from newly installed wood framing, to refurbishing old doors.

Woodwork

A plus gives furniture a new lease on life. Using lacquer and specialty products, we are able to completely transform old furniture into new furniture at a fraction of the cost.

Faux finishing

A plus is one of the few painting companies who also offer a variety of faux finishings. We are experts in venetian plaster (our most common) along with basic textures, marble walls, wood grain and metallic faux finishes. This technique is recommended for accent walls and specialty rooms.
